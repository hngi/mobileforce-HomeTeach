About HomeTeach

HomeTeach is an application platform build to help bridge the gap between quality teaching and tutorial service acquisition. It provides a medium where parents or students can find and hire private tutors within their proximity to foster the learning growth of their children or themselves.

The platform gives students and parents the ability to search for tutors, message them directly within the app, select a schedule for their ward’s classes and book a session. Tutors from different disciplines who are willing to teach at affordable rates can rapidly communicate with parents and students who are interested in booking them and also keep track of all upcoming classes making managing their personal tutoring business even easier.

Parents can view and access tutor details before requesting their service helping them to validate the expertise of their ward’s prospective tutor.
